# BenefitPay Checkout Guide - Kyub Store

## How to Subscribe to Kyub Store Services

Follow these steps to purchase a subscription using BenefitPay.

## Step 1: Go to Checkout

1. Visit **Kyub Store** website
2. Click **اشترك الآن** (Subscribe Now) button
3. You'll be taken to the checkout page at `/checkout`

## Step 2: Enter Your Information

Fill in all required fields (marked with *):

### Personal Information
- **الاسم الكامل** (Full Name): Your complete name
- **رقم الهاتف** (Phone Number): Your WhatsApp or mobile number
- **البريد الإلكتروني** (Email): Your active email address
  - We'll send confirmation and subscription details here
  - Make sure it's correct!

## Step 3: Choose Your Plan

1. Click on **الخطة** (Plan) dropdown
2. Select the service you want:
   - Netflix Premium
   - Crunchyroll
   - ChatGPT Plus
   - TOD
   - VIP Services
   - iPTV
   - Renewals
3. Plan price will display below

### Price Reference
- Prices shown in **البحرين د.ب** (Bahraini Dinar) by default
- You can change currency in the top header
- Prices shown are in your selected currency

## Step 4: Upload Payment Proof

### Getting Your Payment Proof

1. **Payment Methods Accepted:**
   - BenefitPay app
   - BenefitPay online portal
   - Bank transfer (if integrated with BenefitPay)

2. **After Payment:**
   - Take a screenshot of the payment confirmation
   - Make sure it shows:
     - ✓ Successful payment status
     - ✓ Order amount
     - ✓ Date/time
     - ✓ Receipt or confirmation code (optional)

### Upload the Image

1. Click on the **upload area** (or "تغيير الصورة" to change)
2. Select the payment proof image from your device
3. Requirements:
   - **Format**: JPG, PNG, WebP, or other image formats
   - **Size**: Maximum 5MB
   - **Content**: Clear, readable payment confirmation

4. **Image Preview:**
   - Image displays once uploaded
   - Review to ensure it's correct
   - Click "تغيير الصورة" to change if needed

## Step 5: Optional - Enter BenefitPay Reference

If your payment confirmation shows a reference number:
- **رقم إحالة BenefitPay** (Optional)
- Copy the reference code from your BenefitPay confirmation
- This helps us verify your payment faster
- **Not required** - payment proof image is enough

## Step 6: Review Your Order

Before submitting, verify:

- ✓ Name is spelled correctly
- ✓ Phone number is correct (we'll contact here if needed)
- ✓ Email is correct (confirmation sent here)
- ✓ Selected plan is what you want
- ✓ Price matches what you paid
- ✓ Payment proof is clear and readable

## Step 7: Submit Your Order

1. Click **تم الدفع / إرسال الطلب** (Payment Done / Submit Order)
2. Process may take a few seconds
3. **Success Message:**
   - "تم استقبال طلبك بنجاح"
   - "Order received successfully"
   - "سيتم التحقق من الدفع وإرسال تفاصيل الاشتراك قريباً"
   - "Payment verification and subscription details coming soon"

## What Happens Next?

### Timeline

| When | What |
|------|------|
| ✓ Immediately | Order received in our system |
| ✓ 5 minutes | Confirmation email sent |
| 1-4 hours | Admin verifies payment proof |
| 1-24 hours | Approval or rejection email |
| Upon approval | Subscription credentials sent |

### After Submission

1. **Confirmation Email:**
   - Check your inbox (check spam folder too!)
   - Includes order number and details
   - Acknowledges we received your order

2. **Wait for Approval:**
   - Admin reviews payment proof
   - May take 1-24 hours
   - You'll get approval or rejection email

3. **Upon Approval:**
   - Receive subscription login details
   - Includes username and password
   - May include access link
   - Instructions for first login

### Upon Rejection (if needed)

- Email explains why payment was rejected
- You can resubmit new payment proof
- Or choose different payment method
- Contact support if unclear

## Troubleshooting

### Image Won't Upload
**Try:**
1. Use different image format (JPG instead of PNG)
2. Resize image if larger than 5MB
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Use mobile if browser has issues

### Didn't Receive Confirmation Email
**Check:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Check email address - is it correct?
3. Wait 5 minutes (might be delayed)
4. Contact support with your order details

### Form Shows Errors
**Fix:**
1. Make sure all * fields are filled
2. Email must be valid (example@mail.com format)
3. Phone must be entered
4. Image must be selected
5. Plan must be chosen

### Payment Proof Rejected
**Then:**
1. Check rejection reason email
2. Common issues:
   - Image not clear enough
   - Amount doesn't match
   - Wrong payment confirmation
3. Resubmit with clearer image
4. Or contact support for help
